Explore Careers

Thank you for your interest in joining the team at Ways Unlimited! We are always searching for passionate and ambitious individuals to join us.

Pilot

Pilots ensure the safety of the passengers and crew members and control all flight decisions once in the air. We are looking for someone who has received training and has earned their aviation license.

Cabin Service Agent

Our aircraft needs to be kept clean after a long flight. Help us sanitize and restock our aircraft! Minimal training is needed, along with a high school diploma.

Airline Customer Service Agent

Want to help make getting on the plane easier than ever before? ACSAs assist passengers with check-in, issues tickets, and checks baggage.

Flight Attendant

Our flight attendants are responsible for ensuring all of our fliers are safe and comfortable throughout their flight. We are searching for highly sociable and attentive candidates. If you meet these requirements, this job might be the one for you!

Sales Representative

Dear skilled persuaders, don’t let your talent go unnoticed! We would love to see passionate individuals spreading our brand to others. All you need is a degree in business, and skills in customer service.

Aircraft Mechanic

Our avionic technicians handle the technical aspects of the aircraft. A certified mechanic with experience would be most fitting for this career!

Co-Pilot

Are you interested in working alongside experienced highly ranked pilots? Further your training by shadowing well-known pilots and become a co-pilot! An aviation license is required; however, someone with little to no experience is most suitable.

Flight Dispatcher

Flight dispatchers assist in preparing a detailed schedule of paths maximizing the efficiency of the flight. Someone who is already received the right amount of education for this career would be most fitting!

Interested In Any Of These Careers?

Don’t let your ablities go to waste! Apply to Ways Unlimited here today! We’re happy to welcome all those who meet the requirements and believe in proudly representing our corporation.
